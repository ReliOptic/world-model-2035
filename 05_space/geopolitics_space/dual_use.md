## Purpose
- This is a concept memo scaffold for dual-use dynamics in space systems.
- It should capture how commercial, civil, and military capabilities overlap without assuming intent from capability alone.
- It should distinguish legal framing, operational dependence, and escalation risk.

## Core question
- Which space infrastructures are becoming systemically dual-use by 2035, and what changes once states depend on them?

## Analytical split
- Communications and network resilience
- ISR and sensing
- Launch and rapid replenishment
- Positioning, timing, and navigation
- Commercial services used for military or strategic purposes

## 2026 baseline questions
- 어떤 상업 시스템이 사실상 안보 인프라로 쓰이고 있는지 확인 필요
- capability overlap과 explicit military integration을 분리할 필요
