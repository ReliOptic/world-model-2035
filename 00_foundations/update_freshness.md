# Update Freshness
**정보 신선도:** 🟢  |  **최종 갱신:** 2026-04  |  **다음 갱신:** 2026-07

## Purpose
Keep topic files current and make stale material visible.

## Freshness labels
- `🟢` updated within the last 3 months
- `🟡` updated 3 to 6 months ago
- `🔴` older than 6 months and should be refreshed before active use

## Required header
All substantive files should declare:

```markdown
**정보 신선도:** 🟢/🟡/🔴  |  **최종 갱신:** YYYY-MM  |  **다음 갱신:** YYYY-MM
```

## Refresh cadence by domain
- Frontier AI and semiconductors: monthly or on major roadmap events
- Geopolitics and regulation: monthly and on major policy actions
- Climate and nature: quarterly unless a major report or event lands
- Economics and labor: monthly for fast-moving policy or market structure topics, otherwise quarterly
- Science and medicine: on material paper, trial, or institutional updates

## When a file must be refreshed immediately
- a key source is older than 6 months
- a milestone year has rolled over
- a central official roadmap changed
- a forecast was invalidated by events

## Review workflow
- check the file header freshness state
- refresh primary sources
- update current-state facts first
- revise forecast only after present-state facts are current
- update `## 정보 출처`
