## Purpose
- This is a concept memo scaffold for the multiplanetary thesis as used in this repository.
- It should distinguish philosophical framing, engineering prerequisites, and institutional feasibility.
- It should not treat aspiration as evidence of timeline credibility.

## Core question
- By 2035, what would count as meaningful progress toward a multiplanetary civilization versus symbolic demonstration only?

## Analytical split
- Vision layer: stated goals, moral framing, civilizational arguments
- Engineering layer: transport, life support, in-situ resource use, reliability, cost
- Governance layer: regulation, treaties, public legitimacy, military spillover
- Economic layer: who pays, who benefits, what persists after initial enthusiasm
