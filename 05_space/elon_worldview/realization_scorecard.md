## Purpose
- This file is a scorecard scaffold for evaluating how much of the Elon-linked space thesis becomes operational reality by 2035.
- It is intended to separate rhetoric, partial execution, and durable system change.
- No score should be assigned without explicit sourcing and dated criteria.

## Scope
- SpaceX execution versus stated long-term ambition
- Starlink as infrastructure versus narrative amplifier
- Multiplanetary framing versus economically durable capability
- Adjacent worldview spillover into AI, defense, and neurotechnology where relevant

- Capability realization
- Economic durability
- Policy/regulatory acceptance
- Strategic dependency created for states or firms
- Degree of narrative overhang relative to achieved infrastructure
