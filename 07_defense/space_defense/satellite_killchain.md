## Purpose
- This is a concept memo scaffold for how satellites become targets, sensors, relays, and dependencies within a military kill chain.
- It should distinguish kinetic, electronic, cyber, and orbital disruption pathways.
- It should not imply current doctrine or capabilities without sourcing.

## Core question
- By 2035, which parts of the satellite kill chain become most operationally decisive and most escalation-prone?

## Research prompts
- 위성 의존도가 높은 군은 어떤 replacement or resilience logic를 가지는가
- 전자전, 사이버, 궤도기동 중 어느 경로가 가장 실전적이며 저위험으로 간주되는가

## Boundaries and cautions
- Separate observed capability from inferred doctrine.
- Note whether a disruption claim is reversible, attributable, and escalatory.
- Keep debris-related second-order effects distinct from immediate battlefield utility.
